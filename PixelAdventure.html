Game sometimes breaks in older Firefox or IE browsers; so try Chrome

Move with WASD or arrow keys. Collect all coins. Black = Dragon. Pink = Fairy. Purple = YOU.
Beware of zombies and fire.
Also, use Number pad to fire in directions different from movement (8 for north, 3 for south-west)
